ABOUT

Nopheros is a voice actor specializing in versatile character performance and commercial delivery. With an age range spanning children through elderly characters and accents including Southern American, Boston, British Cockney, and Slavic dialects, he brings adaptability to projects across animation, video games, audiobooks, and corporate content.

His background in live broadcast performance has developed skills in cold reading, improvisation, timing precision, and vocal endurance. Select credits include promo work for gaming and streaming platforms. Available for remote sessions with quick turnaround and flexible scheduling.
