Booking DJ Nopheros

Signal-boosting atmospheres built to move bodies and minds. Nopheros curates industrial, darkwave, future synth, heavy bass, and cinematic electronic sets with the precision of a systems architect and the intention of a storyteller.

Forged in the virtual landscapes of Anarchy Online, The Matrix Online, and World of Warcraft, Nopheros carried his craft into The Real inside nightclubs across Providence and Boston where each set unfolds like a composed journey. His dynamic arcs fuse lighting, visuals, pacing, and crowd energy into singular performances that grow richer with each transition.

He has brought his distinct presence to stages at PAX East Boston 2025 and supported acclaimed acts including Powerman 5000 and Assemblage 23, earning residencies in Providence and live syndication on Everlook Broadcasting Co.

Signal curious? Then reach out today and book DJ Nopheros for your event, virtual, reality, or even meta-physical!
